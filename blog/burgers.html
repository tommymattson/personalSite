My Top 5 Burgers

April 11, 2026 • 5 min read
Generally, my burger taste is straight forward and simple. Cheese, burger, lettuce, onion, tomato, and some sauce is all that you need. The burger is the working man’s sandwich, perfect for eating a filling meal while keeping a hand free. But burgers also have the capability to rally a group around a table for a great meal and memories. Methodology - Price x Quality: Price is key for me when rating burgers. It may be because I entered the workforce in high school at California’s finest establishment, In-N-Out Burger where freshness and quality do not come at exorbitant pricing. I often reminisce on a time where you could get a double cheeseburger for under $4 and a whole meal for less than $10. You will not see a $20 burger on my list – I am currently morally opposed to order a burger at this price point. I know that eventually burger prices will swell far beyond $20(we will reserve my thoughts and opinions on central bank policy & minimum wage for another day) but today is not that day. I am no food critic but I have some opinions. I do not want this to be a rage bait piece so I will leave out my least favorite burgers. Enjoy and have a burger for me.
